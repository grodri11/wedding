Like every love story, it always starts with one moment that makes your heart skips a beat and your world turns around. Strangely enough, our story began with a simple Facebook message from Alex to Vivi that would change our life forever. Our relationship then filled with long distance calls and virtual commitment between Auckland (GMT +12) and Vancouver(GMT -7) for a good 6 months, before Alex's one month visit to Vancouver during summer 2013 turned into a permanent stay up until now.

Oh yeah, did we mention that our first time meeting each other physically was 3 months after we committed into the relationship? Yes it was! And one funny story, the first thing that came out from Vivi when we met for the first time at Jakarta airport was "You look darker than you were on Facetime". And then we laughed together. That was definitely an ice breaking moment!

Fast forward to 2014 Christmas holiday, Alex proposed to Vivi and luckily she said... 'YES!'. This wedding will definitely be one of the most important moments in our life and we want to share the memories with people that we care the most.
